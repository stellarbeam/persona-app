# The user login flow

Create a login page with phone number and password [2FA]

The user shall be able to select the role he is choosing to sign in/sign up [ Admin, Boss, Employee]

# In the sign-up

The user shall be taken to a screen where he is prompted to fill in his details and pick the role they wish to sign-up for.
For some roles, the users shall be prompted some extra fields.
And next button.

On clicking next, he should be prompted for the OTP (use firebase phone verification).
When verified take them back to Login.


Begin a user onboarding flow on successful Login that gives users a small walkthrough.
Also give them an option to skip it.

Please note:

The application shall have a splash screen.

Users shall be prompted to pick their default language when the application starts, default shall be English
There shall be option to change the language at any point.

Light mode and Dark mode to not hurt my eyes at 2AM ;)
